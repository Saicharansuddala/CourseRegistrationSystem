Why Wildroot

Small cohorts, real field sites, no lecture halls.

We built Wildroot around a simple idea: ecology is best learned outdoors. Every course listed here runs at an active field station, farm, reef, or observatory — not a classroom.

  • Every instructor is an active field researcher in their subject.
  • Class sizes are capped so every student gets hands-on time.
  • Enroll instantly online — no waitlist forms, no office hours required.
  • Credits transfer to most environmental science and biology programs.
